Welcome to My Portfolio

I'm Nick McKinnis, a passionate technical game designer with a deep love for crafting immersive and innovative gaming experiences. My journey into game development began in 2012, where I honed my skills as a narrative designer, weaving stories that captivate and engage. By 2018, my career evolved as I embraced freelancing in technical roles, allowing me to dive into the intricate details of game design.

As a Unity specialist and pixel artist, I've spent countless hours perfecting shaders and developing complex gameplay systems that bring games to life. My expertise has led me to take on the role of Chief Design Officer at Digital Scorpion Interactive, where I've had the privilege of leading a talented team to release Tales Of Valoris on iOS and Android, and launch a demo for our upcoming title Hit N' Bit on Steam. Currently, we're hard at work on an exciting port and expansion of Tales Of Valoris for Steam, pushing the boundaries of what our game can achieve.

But my passion doesn't stop there—I thrive on challenges, which is why I regularly participate in game jams, where creativity and speed are put to the test. Here are a few projects I'm particularly proud of:

  • Revenge of the Tartans: A love letter to the Kingdom series, this project was a 10-day sprint where I was the sole in-engine developer, collaborating with an artist and sound designer to bring it to life.
  • Drawn to Dream: As the lead game designer, project manager, pixel artist, and technical animator, I poured my heart into this puzzle platformer. I handled everything from animation states to pixel art animations, all within a tight 10-day deadline.
  • Cue: This metroidvania, where I was responsible for the artwork, AI, UI, dialogue, cutscenes, and enemy design, earned 13th place—a testament to the power of a small but mighty team.

Each project I work on is a step forward in my quest to create games that resonate with players, combining technical prowess with artistic flair. Thank you for visiting, and I hope my work inspires you as much as it does me!
