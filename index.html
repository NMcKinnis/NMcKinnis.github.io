Welcome to My Portfolio

I'm Nick McKinnis, a passionate technical game designer with a deep love for crafting immersive and innovative gaming experiences. My journey into game development began in 2012, where I honed my skills as a narrative designer, weaving stories that captivate and engage. By 2018, my career evolved as I embraced freelancing in technical roles, allowing me to dive into the intricate details of game design.

As a Unity specialist and pixel artist, I’ve spent countless hours perfecting shaders and developing complex gameplay systems that bring games to life. My expertise has led me to take on the role of Chief Design Officer at Digital Scorpion Interactive, where I've had the privilege of leading a talented team to release Tales Of Valoris on iOS and Android, and launch a demo for our upcoming title Hit N' Bit on Steam. Currently, we’re hard at work on an exciting port and expansion of Tales Of Valoris for Steam, pushing the boundaries of what our game can achieve.
